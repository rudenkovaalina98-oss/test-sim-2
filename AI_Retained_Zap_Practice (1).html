Practice

You've already given this journey your time, your attention, and your heart. This module invites you into something real — a retained client practice. Not a trial. A deeper, longer conversation where your ability to hold space, maintain warmth, and create genuine connection will be tested.

Think of it as practicing what it feels like to work with a real returning client — someone who came back because she needs to be heard.

You can repeat this practice as many times as you need. Each session is a fresh opportunity to grow, refine your approach, and deepen your skills.
You'll have a session with Annabelle — a client who carries deep anxiety about love and loneliness. Before the chat begins, take a quiet moment with her background. Let yourself understand who she is, what she might be feeling beneath her words, and how you'd like to meet her.

There is no time limit in this practice. The longer you keep Annabelle engaged, the better your result. Quality connection over time is the goal. When you feel the session has reached a natural close — or when you're ready to wrap up — type END SESSION in the chat. The bot will generate your personal feedback and score. You can then start a new session whenever you're ready.
  • 🔮 Reading Depth — Did you go beyond a simple yes/no? Annabelle needs to feel you truly looked into her situation. One clear, grounded insight can feel powerful when it's sincere and specific.
  • 💜 Empathy — Did you make her feel seen in her loneliness? When you reflect her emotions with care and precision, trust is built — and she stays.
  • 🎤 Tone of Voice — Was your style warm, professional, and appropriate for an esoteric advisor? Tone shapes the entire experience.
  • ⏳ Timefillers — Did you expand your messages to maintain flow and duration? Short, rushed answers close the conversation. Rich, spacious responses keep it alive.
  • 🔗 Engagement — Did you use thoughtful questions and gentle hooks to prevent her from drifting away? Keeping her curious and involved is the heart of retention.
This moment happens only once. Take a slow breath before you begin. The longer you hold the space with intention, the more you grow. You are ready.
